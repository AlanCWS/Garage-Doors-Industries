About A Plus Garage Doors LLC

Our mission

We're passionate about delivering an exceptional experience to our customers. When you hire us, you're hiring a team of professionals who are trained and skilled to deliver lasting value to your home. From the initial inspection to the completion of the job , we consider ourselves humble guests in your home, and, as such, respect your personal privacy and space. We apply the most innovative and cutting-edge designs in today's market. You can rely on our professional team to make recommendations that fit your taste and style, but we will always give you the final word.

Our garage door service

We have been working in the garage door industry for over 20 years, and we strongly believe that what goes around comes around, and what you sow is what you reap. We have always known that conducting our business with honesty and with the utmost respect towards our customers will always lead to more customers. We'll personally make sure that you feel comfortable recommending us to your friends and family. Please give us the opportunity to help you with your garage door, and you will not be disappointed. Thank you in advance for trusting us with your garage door needs. We look forward to meeting with you.

Why choose us

From large-scale commercial projects to home garage door repair, no project is too big or small that it doesn't get our full attention. We are dedicated, and we thank our clients for recognizing our hard work with their referrals and repeat business.

Long-term business

We are a family-owned and operated business that is licensed, bonded, and insured. We have more than 17 years of experience serving Phoenix and surrounding areas. We are an A+-rated member of the BBB and work with all major brands and models in the garage door industry. We have the latest products and technology and offer free in-home estimates on garage door replacement. Honest, reliable, and affordable service without compromising the quality of our products or customer service gives us the opportunity to prove that we honor our name, A Plus Garage Doors LLC.

Leave it to us.

Whether you're looking for garage door maintenance, repair, or part replacement, we have the knowledge and expertise to provide timely, affordable, and professional service. For more information, please contact us today.

Our promise to you

We promise to always provide value and outstanding service, regardless of your lifestyle need, design preference, or budget constraint.
